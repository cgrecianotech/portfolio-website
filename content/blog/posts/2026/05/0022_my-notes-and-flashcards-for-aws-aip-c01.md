## About my Notes for AIP-C01
The AWS AIP-C01 exam validates a candidate's ability to effectively integrate foundation models (FMs) into applications and business workflows. This certification demonstrates practical knowledge of how to implement GenAI solutions into production environments by using AWS technologies.

<img src="{{ '0022_aip-notes-content.png' | blogImagePath }}" alt="Table of Contents of my Study Notes for AWS AIP-C01" eleventy:ignore>

My notes cover the knowledge domains comprehensively. You will dive deep into Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), vector stores, and Agentic AI, and learn the nuances of AWS products in the context of GenAI. Amazon Bedrock is the main focus of the exam, particularly Bedrock Knowledge Bases, Bedrock AgentCore, and Bedrock Guardrails. There is a **free** version of my notes which is hosted online in a Notion site, and there is a paid PDF version which you can purchase in [my Ko-Fi shop](https://ko-fi.com/christiangreciano/shop) (useful for printing, studying offline, etc).

<img src="{{ '0022_aip-notes-shop-screenshot.png' | blogImagePath }}" alt="Screenshot of my AIP-C01 Study Notes PDF in my Ko-Fi shop" eleventy:ignore>

My materials assume you have already passed the preceding AWS AI certifications, namely [AWS AI Practitioner (AIF-C01)](https://aws.amazon.com/certification/certified-ai-practitioner/) and [AWS ML Engineer Associate (MLA-C01)](https://aws.amazon.com/certification/certified-machine-learning-engineer-associate/). If you haven't done so yet, I really recommend you do so, because the professional-level AIP-C01 builds upon those. You can find notes and flashcards of those preceding certs also in [my website](https://christiangreciano.com/) if you need them!

The main source of my materials is the [Udemy course by Frank Kane and Stéphane Maarek for this certification](https://www.udemy.com/course/ultimate-aws-certified-generative-ai-developer-professional/?couponCode=KEEPLEARNING), although I have also included some information from other websites and AWS blogs. I took this video course when I prepared for the exam, and I can highly recommend it to complement my materials. Both Kane and Maarek are highly regarded instructors in the AWS community. Note: The video course contains a lot of content from preceding certifications which I have not included in my materials, since I already have that content in my other notes.
